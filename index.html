About me

A modern and innovative software developer who loves spending much of his time crafting web and mobile applications to solve business problems. With a full stack web development certification from FreeCodeCamp and 2+ years of experience in the tech industry, I promise to deliver the best quality of work in a reasonable amount of time.

Prior to 2019, I focused on front end web development to bring the best experience to your users. As I have mostly worked on the front end side, I am familiar with using BEM architecture, Bootstrap, React and Redux to bring the best interactive experience to users.

The increased demand for a know-all web developer has driven up my motivation to work on relational and non-relational databases, RESTful API, and GraphQL. As such, I have become a full stack web developer and worked on many e-commerce side projects.

Besides this, my set of projects also sees mobile applications, the most complex and latest of which is Cash Register. I am developing this iOS app for a private company using Swift, Cocoatouch frameworks, and many more.

Cash Register

A cash register project for a coffee store

A delicate and interactive Cash Register compatiable with iPad platforms with intuitive user interface design

In this project, I was able to connect the iOS app with peripheral devices over WiFi, a cash drawer and two thermal printers. One of them was responsible for printing temporary receipts, and the other was in charge of printing a receipt proof of payment.

Other than that, we also helped the staff members with adding products to the machine by implementing a barcode scanner which boosted the speed and accuracy of the process. A credit card scanner was also added to retrieve the information of credit cards efficiently

Tech stack: Swift, Objective C (partly), Cocoa Touch Frameworks, MySQL, RestFul APIs
